Ten Of The Worlds Best Amusement Parks

Posted on February 15, 2015 by Matt Robertson

Planning a family vacation and wondering where to head to? If your kids are into amusement parks, here are some suggestions for you:

1. Cedar Point, Sandusky, Ohio, USA

Home to a total of 69 attractions, among which 17 roller coasters and the best roller coaster in the world (Millenium Force), Cedar Point is the world’s best theme park, according to Amusement Today.

2. Europa Park, Rust, Germany

The best European theme park offers 55 attractions, including 11 roller coasters and Silver Star – the second highest roller coaster in Europe (73 meters).

3. Dollywood, Pigeon Forge, Tennessee, USA

This amusement park is partly owned by the American singer Dolly Parton, nicknamed the Queen of Country Music in the USA. Dollywood is home to one of the best wooden roller coasters in the world – Thunderhead.

4. Knoebels Amusement Resort, Elysburg, Pennsylvania, USA

Knoebels Amusement Resort is renowned for its two wooden roller coasters (Phoenix, ranked 2nd best wooden rollercoaster in the world), the Haunted House (ranked 4th best in the world) and for the quality of its food outlets.

5. Disneyland, Anaheim, California, USA

The first Disney Park, and the only one designed and seen by Walt Disney during his lifetime, this theme parks is home to popular attractions such as Indiana Jones and the Temple of the Forbidden Eye – a scenic route in the dark.

6. Busch Gardens Williamsburg, Williamsburg, Virginia, USA

This park’s visitors appreciate its landscapes, its food, its cleanliness and its shows. Busch Gardens Williamsburg is known as the most beautiful theme park in the world.

7. Magic Kingdom, Orlando, Florida, USA

Magic Kingdom is Orlando’s version of Disneyland – a classic and one of the most visited sights of the city.

8. Universal Island of Adventure, Orlando, Florida, USA

This theme park’s attractions are based on Universal movies. Among the most famous – the scenic routes Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey, Amazing Adventures of Spider-Man and one of the best water circuits of the world Dudley Do-Right’s Ripsaw Falls.

9. Tokyo DisneySea, Tokyo, Japan

The best Asian theme park, DisneySea it is part of Tokyo Disney Resort, together with the Tokyo Disneyland. This is one of the most beautiful Disney Parks and perhaps the most successful in terms of decor.

10. Blackpool Pleasure Beach, Blackpool, England, UK

The 2nd best European amusement park, after Europa Park, Blackpool Pleasure Beach is home to 56 attractions, including the aquatic one: Valhalla.
